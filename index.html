A Brief Introduction

I love science.
Starting in middle school, I already learnt a lot from doing chemical experiments.
I also love studying physics after I entered high school,
which also costs me two years to study for the Chines Physics Olympaid.
Although I am not studying in the field of natural science anymore,
I do find my interest in the computing science,
which is a more theoretical part of the subject:
computing theory and algorithm design.

After I entered college, I found myself interested in film photography.
To take film photos, it requires one to know the concept of shutter, the aperture etc.
I love taking photos in film not only because it is a relatively old form of art,
but also because it can preserve a physical copy of photo on the film,
which can be rarely seen inside a digital world.

From the day I took my first film photo,
I start to take on a journey of recording my path of life,
those unforgettable moments of my life and the people that are important to me.

I also love music.
Music is the common language of mankind, and it is really beautiful when you play it,
or even just listen to it.
I learnt how to play GuZheng when I was 8 years old and I only learnt it for 2 years.
I quit learning GuZheng because I am not a big fan of traditional Chinies music.
After that, I learnt how to play guitar and ukulele by myself.
When I was year 2 student in college, I start to wonder, why not learn an instrument that
could let you perform in an orchestra?
With that thought hold in mind, I start to learn violin and until now I am still learning it.
